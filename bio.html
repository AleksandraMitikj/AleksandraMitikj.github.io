My story

I'm Aleksandra Mitic and I come from Macedonia. I'm 20 years old and I can be your ski instructor. Skiing is my first love. I got on skis when I was only three years old, and already at four I was going down the slopes independently. Skiing is my greatest passion, and this passion led me to become a professional ski instructor.

With years of experience and dedication, I have learned a lot about the techniques and skills needed for successful skiing. As a ski instructor, I share my love for skiing with my students and help them improve their skills and confidence on the slopes.

Each lesson with me is tailored to the needs and level of the student, ensuring an enjoyable and safe experience. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced skier, my focus is on your progress and enjoyment of this wonderful sport.
